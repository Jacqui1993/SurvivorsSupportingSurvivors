About us page

Have you ever been told by a nurse/Doctor; "You will never walk or talk again.."? If so, We may be able to help you!

We are a voluntary organisation who have lived through a brain injury and came out the other side. We are here to help fellow brain injury survivors by being able to empathise with you as we have been there. Just because you have been told what you might not be able to do doesn't mean that will happen.

Our activites will promote equality snd diversity in promoting an understanding of brain injuries among the public and other organisations/agencies and raising awareness of the importance of meeting the needs and rights of survivors of brain injuries.
